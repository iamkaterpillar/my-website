About Me

Hi, I'm Kat! I'm a California-raised girl living in London. I moved here for my MBA at London Business School, met my husband while working at Wintermute, and somehow got stuck in the UK (for now).

By day, I work in product at a crypto startup called Bebop. I’m not technical (yet), but I keep ending up as the only non-engineer in very technical teams. I like to think it’s because I’m the glue that keeps things running — or maybe just because I’m a bit obsessed with structure, lists, and solving chaos. I’m a zero-inbox person, always organizing, and never without a to-do list.

This site is a little experiment. I’m setting it up to learn how to make a website — and maybe share a few things I’ve learned along the way, so I don’t forget them.
