About me

Hiya, I'm Kat! I'm a California-raised girl living in London. I moved here for my MBA at London Business School, met my husband while working at Wintermute, and stayed to build cool things with magic internet money.

Life is 10% what happens to you and 90% how you react to it. So try to make the best of it!

I just started a new job as Partner Marketing Lead at Ondo Finance. Previously, I led product for a crypto trading app called Bebop. I'm not an engineer, but I'm usually surrounded by them - both at work and outside of it. I like to think it's because I'm the glue that keeps things running… or maybe just because I'm a bit obsessed with structure, lists, and bringing order to chaos. I'm a zero-inbox kind of person, always organizing, and never without a to-do list.

This site is a little experiment. I'm setting it up to learn how to build a website - and maybe to share some things I've learned in my career and along the way, so I don't forget them.
