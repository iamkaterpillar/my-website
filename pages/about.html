About me

Hiya, I'm Kat! I'm a California-raised girl living in London. I moved here for my MBA at London Business School, met my husband while working at Wintermute, and now we live in the UK (for now).

I'm a firm believer in what is meant to be will be. Life is 10% what happens to you and 90% how you react to it.

I work in product at a crypto startup called Bebop. I'm not an engineer (or even close), but I'm usually surrounded by engineers - both at work and outside of it. I like to think it's because I'm the glue that keeps things running… or maybe just because I'm a bit obsessed with structure, lists, and bringing order to chaos. I'm a zero-inbox kind of person, always organizing, and never without a to-do list.

This site is a little experiment. I'm setting it up to learn how to build a website — and maybe to share some things I've learned in my career and along the way, so I don't forget them.
