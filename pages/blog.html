Vibe Coding iamkaterpillar

"Vibe coding" isn't a formal programming term - it's more of a mood, a mindset, and an energy.

It's what happens when you're coding without a rigid plan — not because you have to, but because it feels fun or interesting. You're exploring, tinkering, prototyping — just following what feels right.

I'm just trying to document some of my learnings here as I create this website. AI agents are definitely changing the game.
